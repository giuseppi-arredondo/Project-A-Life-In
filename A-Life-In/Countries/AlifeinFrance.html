France, located in Western Europe, is known for its rich history, cultural heritage, fashion, and cuisine. Paris is the capital and largest city, with a population of approximately 67 million people. French is the official language, and the currency is the Euro (EUR). France operates as a unitary semi-presidential republic.

People are attracted to living in France for its diverse landscapes, iconic landmarks (such as the Eiffel Tower and Louvre Museum), high standard of living, and strong emphasis on arts, culture, and gastronomy. Notable personalities from France include Napoleon Bonaparte (emperor), Coco Chanel (fashion designer), and Zinedine Zidane (footballer).

France's minimum wage in 2024 is approximately 1,689 EUR per month (around 1,900 USD). The healthcare system in France is considered one of the best in the world, offering universal coverage. Education is free and compulsory from ages 3 to 16, emphasizing cultural and intellectual development.
