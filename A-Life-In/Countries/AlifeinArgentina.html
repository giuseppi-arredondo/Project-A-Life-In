Argentina, located in South America, is the eighth largest country in the world by area, with a population of approximately 45 million people. The capital and largest city is Buenos Aires. Spanish is the official language, and the currency is the Argentine peso (ARS). Argentina is a federal republic with a presidential system of government.

People are attracted to living in Argentina due to its rich cultural heritage, diverse landscapes, and vibrant cities. The country offers a high quality of life with a strong emphasis on arts, music, and sports. Notable personalities from Argentina include Lionel Messi, Pope Francis, and Jorge Luis Borges.

Argentina's minimum wage in 2024 is around 130,000 ARS per month, roughly 550 USD depending on the exchange rate. The healthcare system combines public and private services, offering free universal access to basic care.
